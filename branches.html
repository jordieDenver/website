The Grand Fleet of Euphoria



This branch is the main bulk and the backbone of the clan.
Every new member is placed into this branch and by proving themselves and showing interest,
they can move to any other branch in the clan or work to take on leadership roles.
The Grand Fleet of Euphoria is currently ran by True Gxldxn.


The Department of Creation



This branch is where the creative minds of the clan go.
The people in the Department of Creation are tasked to build maps for the clan and to design media.
The branch is also special in the sense that you can be in the DOC at the same time as any other branch. The Department of Creation is currently ran by CandiedConnor.


The Zenith



This branch is for the competitive side of the clan.
The word 'Zenith' stands for 'the point of culmination' or 'the peak'.
The members of the Zenith are tasked to handle any threats to the group in game and to win wars for the clan. The Zenith is currently ran by Technolxgy with CandiedConnor as the co-leader.
