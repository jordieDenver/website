Hello, and welcome to Euphoria.
Our clan was founded in December, 2021 by BillyBo Bats and Perciev.
Since our foundation, Euphoria's goal has been to provide a vibrant atmosphere free from the drama and toxicity of the general community.
Euphoria aims to be a beacon within the community and remove tyranny wherever we see it.
Here we uphold a friendly environment and a place for everyone.
Members of Euphoria are given activities, special events, and a healthy RP experience to grow and develop as a player.
Whether a casual or competitive player, everyone has a place here.
We look forward to serving with you all on the battlefield.
